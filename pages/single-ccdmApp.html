App for "CCDM" design talks

2015
APP | Branding | Video

This is a mobile app for an annual design conference “CCDM“ with the participation of different professionals that work on this subject. The conference “CCDM“ has a different graphic identity every year! So I tried to produce a flexible app that could visually adapt annually. // Images used from the real event (from 2013 to 2015)



















Adaptative color filter

The app changes its color to the identity of each year. The app picks the predominant color of the logotype proposed to that year
