DIWHY? tv channel

2015
Video | Website | Identity

The brief for this project was to create a tv channel (name, concept, channel programming,...) and develop an identity/brand and all the promotional material needed. It was decided that the channel would be a educational fun channel that would help people to be more independent at performing some crafts (cooking, mechanics, gardening,...). The idea consisted on breaking the stereotype image of DIY and creating a strong and autosuficiente message.
