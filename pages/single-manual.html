Manual to Digital

2015
Magazine | Website | Illustration | Collage

The aim of this project was to produce a hybrid magazine (physical and digital format) based on any theme present on the historical catalogues of "The Whole Earth Catalogue". The magazine is made from students to students with the purpose of exchanging knowledge about design and technology.
The process started with choosing 15 articles from the catalogues wich I decided to categorize in 3 main sections: crafts, representation, devices. The name "manual to digital" appeared with this sequence of sections that shows the progression from manual to digital design. The magazine has two formats that are connected by the editorial because one needs the other to be read.

All photos, illustrations and code was developed by me.I do not own the texts from the articles (took from "The whole earth catalogue").
