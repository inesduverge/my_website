Literature Icon

2014
Icon | Vector | Representation

This project consisted on developing the graphic identity of the anual talks "Ciclo de Conversas Design e Multimédia" (CCDM). This event gathers different design professionals to talk about their work and experience in the design field. The pruposal theme for this year image was "Encounter time" so we created a logo that is only readable in a particular perspective when looking to it — it's the meeting of perspectives at a particular moment in time.

Developed by: Inês Duvergé (me); Adriana Nunes.
